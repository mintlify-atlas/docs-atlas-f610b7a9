The analytics page at `/analytics` gives you a visual overview of your spending across any time window you choose. Every chart on the page reacts to the same date range selector, so changing the period updates all views at once. The dashboard is designed to let you quickly spot where your money goes, how your pace compares to your budget, and which days or categories drive your spending.

## Date range filtering

Three range modes are available at the top of the page:

- **Month** — select any month and year. Defaults to the current month.
- **Year** — select a calendar year. Charts aggregate data by month.
- **Custom** — pick a start and end date with the calendar pickers.

## Navigating to analytics

Click **Analytics** in the bottom navigation bar from any protected page, or navigate directly to `/analytics`. Use the back arrow in the header to return to the home dashboard.
