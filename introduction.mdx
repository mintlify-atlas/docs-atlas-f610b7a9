Kashio is a full-stack expense tracker built around a simple idea: logging what you spend should be as easy as saying it out loud. Type `coffee $6`, speak your expense, or message your Telegram bot — Kashio's AI parses the input, extracts the amount, category, and date, and saves it automatically. When you want answers, ask the AI assistant questions like "how much did I spend on food last month?" and get streaming, context-aware responses.

## What Kashio does

Kashio handles the full expense-tracking lifecycle — from capture to insight — without requiring you to fill in forms or remember categories.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Natural language capture" icon="message-lines">
    Enter expenses in plain English. The AI extracts amount, category, description, and date — even relative ones like "yesterday" or "last Tuesday".
  </Card>
  <Card title="Voice input" icon="microphone">
    Speak your expense or chat question. Kashio transcribes audio via Groq Whisper and feeds it into the same parsing pipeline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Analytics dashboard" icon="chart-line">
    View spending over time, category breakdowns, budget vs. actual comparisons, and day-of-week patterns across any date range.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Telegram bot" icon="paper-plane">
    Log expenses and ask spending questions directly from Telegram. Low-confidence parses surface inline confirm/edit actions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with email/password, GitHub, or Google OAuth. Complete the onboarding wizard to set your monthly budget, currency, and expense categories.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your first expense">
    On the home dashboard, type something like `lunch $12` or `uber to airport $42 yesterday` in the expense input. Kashio's AI parses it and saves it — or shows a confirmation dialog if it needs your review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your spending">
    Visit the Analytics page to see charts and breakdowns. Use the AI Chat to ask questions like "What was my biggest expense category in March?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Telegram (optional)">
    Link your Telegram account from **Profile → Integrations** and log expenses or ask questions from anywhere via your phone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tech stack

Kashio is built with Next.js 16, React 19, PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM, Better Auth, and Groq-powered AI (Whisper for transcription, GPT OSS 120B for parsing and chat). See the [self-hosting guide](/deployment/self-hosting) to run it yourself.
